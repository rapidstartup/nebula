## Target List: HOAs

### Where to Find Prospects
1. **Community Associations Institute (CAI)** - National HOA organization
2. **State/county HOA registries** - Public records
3. **Property management companies** - They manage multiple HOAs
4. **Local real estate associations** - Networking opportunities
5. **Online directories:**
   - HOA-USA.com
   - HOA Community Directory
   - State-specific registries

### Priority Targets
- HOAs with documented voting/participation issues
- Large HOAs (500+ units) with complex governance
- HOAs in tech-heavy areas (SF Bay, Seattle, Austin, etc.)
- Newer developments with younger residents
- HOAs with online presence (active Facebook groups, etc.)

---

## Target List: Cooperatives

### National Organizations to Partner With
- **National Cooperative Business Association (NCBA CLUSA)**
- **U.S. Federation of Worker Cooperatives**
- **National Association of Housing Cooperatives**
- **Agricultural cooperative federations** (by state)
- **Credit Union National Association (CUNA)**

### Regional Targets
- **New York City** - Large co-op housing market
- **Bay Area** - Tech worker co-ops
- **Minnesota/Wisconsin** - Strong cooperative tradition
- **Spain** - Mondragon Corporation network
- **UK** - Co-operatives UK membership

### Types to Prioritize
1. **Worker cooperatives** - Tech-savvy, democratic values
2. **Housing cooperatives** - Clear governance needs
3. **Agricultural co-ops** - Geographic spread, member voting
4. **Platform cooperatives** - Already digital-native

## Demo Script: HOAs (10 minutes)

### Opening (1 min)
"How many hours did you spend last year counting ballots, chasing quorum, or dealing with disputed votes?"

### Problem Agitation (2 min)
- Show traditional pain points
- Share stats on low turnout
- Highlight security concerns

### Solution Demo (5 min)
1. Create sample HOA (30 sec)
2. Add properties/members (1 min)
3. Create a vote (1 min)
4. Show resident voting experience (1 min)
5. Display results/dashboard (1.5 min)

### Value Proposition (2 min)
- ROI calculator
- Time savings
- Participation improvements
- Security benefits

### Close
"Shall we set up your free pilot?"

## Demo Script: Cooperatives (15 minutes)

### Opening (2 min)
"Cooperatives are democracy in action. But democratic participation requires tools that match your values."

### Principles Alignment (3 min)
Walk through how Nebula embodies cooperative principles:
- Democratic control (one member, one vote)
- Economic participation (transparent treasury)
- Autonomy (self-hosted option, data ownership)
- Education (voting history, proposal context)

### Live Demo (7 min)
1. Create cooperative structure (1 min)
2. Add member categories (1 min)
3. Submit board election proposal (2 min)
4. Show voting process (2 min)
5. Display results and analytics (1 min)

### Cooperative-Specific Features (3 min)
- Member verification
- Delegation/proxy voting
- Working groups
- Multi-stakeholder governance
- Bylaw integration

### Close
"When can we pilot this with your membership?"
